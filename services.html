What is a commercial court?
A commercial court deals with business-related disputes, including intellectual property, contract enforcement, and other financial disputes.
How to change my profile picture?
To change your profile picture, go to your profile, select 'Edit Profile', and upload a new image.
Where can I see my solved cases?
You can view your solved cases in the 'MY CASES' section of your account dashboard.
